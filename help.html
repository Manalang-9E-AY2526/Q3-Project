How to Use the Fare Matrix

  1. Select your pickup station.
  2. Select your destination station.
  3. Click the calculate button.
  4. The system will compute your MRT-3 fare automatically.
  5. You may also apply discounts applicable to you.

How to Create an Account

  1. Go to the Registration Page: Click the Register button in the header.
  2. Fill in Your Details: Enter your full name, email, and create a secure password.
  3. Upload a Profile Picture (Optional): Use the Choose File button to select a picture from your device.
  4. Agree to Terms: Make sure to check the box agreeing to our terms and conditions.
  5. Submit: Click the Register button at the bottom of the form.
  6. Confirmation: You will see a confirmation message. You can now log in using your email and password.
